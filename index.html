The Problem

Insurance Operations Are Still Manual

P&C insurers lose millions annually to manual review bottlenecks, inconsistent decisions, and delayed collections. AI exists — but no platform connects it natively to Guidewire and Duck Creek workflows.

⚠ The Status Quo

  • Adjusters manually review 50+ claims per day with no AI pre-screening
  • Underwriting decisions are inconsistent across teams and geographies
  • Collections teams cannot predict which accounts to prioritize
  • Guidewire and Duck Creek run as siloed systems with no AI decision layer
  • No explainable AI audit trail for regulatory compliance
  • AI integration projects cost millions and take 12–18 months

✓ The KGA AEGIS Solution

  • AI pre-screens every claim: risk score, fraud signals, severity, and recommended action — before the adjuster opens the file
  • Multi-agent crew delivers structured, documented underwriting decisions consistently
  • Automated collections risk scoring with AI-generated outreach strategy per account
  • Native webhook integration for Guidewire and Duck Creek — no middleware required
  • Every AI decision is tenant-scoped, cached, and tied to a user action with full audit trail
  • SaaS on Azure — deploy in days, not months; plug in any LLM
🌍

LLM-Agnostic by Design

KGA AEGIS is built on a pluggable multi-provider AI abstraction layer. Carriers and MGAs can connect any LLM — today or as the market evolves — without re-architecting their insurance workflows. No vendor lock-in on AI.

The Platform

Three Agentic Sub-Platforms.
One Unified Insurance AI Layer.

Deploy one sub-platform or all three. Each is a standalone application with its own AI engine, adjuster or underwriter workspace UI, and integration adapters — and each accepts any LLM backend.

🛡
Sub-Platform 1

Claims Aegis

AI-powered adjuster workspace that pre-screens every P&C claim through a 4-agent sequential pipeline before the adjuster opens the file.

  • Risk Scoring Agent — financial exposure quantification
  • Fraud Detection Agent — pattern analysis with evidence trail
  • Severity Prediction Agent — reserve estimation
  • Orchestration Agent — recommended action with reasoning
  • Guidewire ClaimCenter & Duck Creek integration
  • Adjuster workspace modeled on industry-standard UI patterns
✓ MVP Complete — May 2026
📋
Sub-Platform 2

Policy Aegis

Underwriter workspace powered by a multi-agent crew and state-machine workflow for consistent, documented renewal and new business decisions.

  • LangGraph state-machine underwriting workflow
  • CrewAI 3-agent underwriting crew with role-based reasoning
  • Rule-based underwriting engine + AI overlay
  • Policy portfolio dashboard with renewal scoring
  • Guidewire PolicyCenter & Duck Creek Policy integration
⚡ In Development
💳
Sub-Platform 3

Billing Aegis

Collections AI that scores accounts by risk, generates AI-driven outreach strategy, and provides premium breakdown analysis for billing teams.

  • Collections risk scoring (payment history, exposure, days past due)
  • AI-generated outreach strategy per account
  • Premium breakdown calculator
  • Billing portfolio dashboard
  • Guidewire BillingCenter & Duck Creek Billing integration
⚡ In Development
